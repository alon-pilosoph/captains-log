The Game

Alone Among the Stars is a solo RPG (role-playing game) by Takuma Okada. In this game, you play a lone explorer, hopping from planet to planet while recording your discoveries in a journal. It is a game of creative writing and solitary self-reflection. TEST

Each planet houses 1 to 6 things to discover, represented in the game by a six-sided die. The prompt for each discovery is randomized by a standard deck of cards. The explorer keeps rolling dice and drawing cards until they are ready to go back home.

This web app is intended to function as your captain's log during your travels. You can log your descoveries and review them in the archive.

Please consider supporting Takuma Okada on itch.io.

The App

This simple web app is an exercise in Flask. It utilizes Bootstrap, relies on Jinja templates and uses Flask extensions such as Flask-SQLAlchemy, Flask-WTF, Flask-Login and Flask-Mail. The app connects to a PostgresSQL database and deployed on Heroku.
